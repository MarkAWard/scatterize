Welcome to scatterize!

Scatterize is an experiment created by Nate Vack, from the Waisman Laboratory for Brain Imaging and Behavior at the University of Wisconsin—Madison.

Crafted from old-growth Python, scatterize has accents of Numpy, Scipy, and scikits.statsmodels. It is set in a framework of the finest Flask, and finished with a mixture of jQuery and an obscure variety of Protovis. The code for scatterize is available on github.

I hope you find this useful! If you do, or have any feedback, send me an email.
